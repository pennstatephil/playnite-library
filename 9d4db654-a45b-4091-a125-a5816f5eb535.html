Description

Welcome home, contestant!

You have proven yourself an exemplary devotee, and therefore have been selected to participate in a series of games curated by those who view us from above. This is an honor of high degree, to be a human participant in an ethereal show. Be very proud of yourself!

Uh oh, there is a catch! Angels, being extra-dimensional beings, are very hard to comprehend for your puny human mind, skin, and body. That means if you take too long you will melt! So get out of this place as soon as possible! (But do try and get a high score!)

Play several exciting minigames to win it all!

  • Hunt a demon!
  • Type for your life!
  • Reach into a pit of plants!
  • Find secret angel passcodes!
  • RUN.
  • Jump through a hoop!
  • Spin a wheel!

Controls

  • WASD - Move
  • Mouse - Look
  • E - Examine
  • Escape - Pause

Extra

Headphones recommended! This game has audio hints which makes certain things easier!

This game has a time slowing feature! In the case that you find this game too difficult, you can change the speed of time at any point to better suit your need.
