Maneuver a marble through obstacles to the end of the level in this PS1 styled, Marble Maze type game.

Why I made this game:

There where several reasons why I made a game like this. The first reason was, that I wanted to try out a more physics based game.

Inspiration:

I had several inspirations for making this game, one of them was those Marble Maze games I played as a child.
The other one was a game called Tama: Adventurous Ball in Giddy Labyrinth that came out as a start title for the PS1 and the Sega Saturn.
