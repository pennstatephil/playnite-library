Viral Reload supports monitor's of every shape and size! You can play with a variety of borders to spruce up the edges of the screen, or focus with a black void. Only a few borders are pictured here, with more to come!

This game is a heavily upgraded version of Project Sanitize, a game I released mid 2020 and was never happy with. This new version has been ported to Godot and heavily reworked with more enemies, refined gameplay, and polish up the wazoo.
