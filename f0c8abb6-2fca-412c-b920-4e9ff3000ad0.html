Description

EMUUROM is a nonviolent creature-scanning metroidvania.



  • Jump into the virtual homeworld of creatures known as EMUUROM!
  • Explore a dense, interconnected land brimming with surprises around every corner.




  • Document your findings in the EMUUDEX.
  • Encounter unseen EMUUROM, and witness how the tree of life branches out!



  • Study the local wildlife with a data-gathering scanner beam.
  • Use the scanner to transform the world around you and create new pathways!


  • Cooperate with a cavalcade of creatures and make use of their systemic behaviour to get around!
  • Some are less willing to help than others...


  • In the horizon, an approaching comet is getting closer by the minute.
  • Can you save the EMUUROM before it's too late?
