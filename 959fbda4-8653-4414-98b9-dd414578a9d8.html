Description

Welcome to Muffin Fight! A high action multiplayer game developed for Virtual Reality!

Get into the game, take on your friends, or play against strangers. Players can bake muffins using many different recipes to create many different effects, and the person with the most points, wins!

What is it?

Muffin Fight is the ultimate party game / food fight (no clean-up required!)

Features

  • Wanting to just get your bake on?  Chill with your friends in the Social space (new v6.3)
  • Do you have what it takes to beat everyone?  Try our Quick play (4 player multiplayer, single player challenge or bots modes)
  • Wanna play with your friends?  Check out Custom matches
  • New v4.6: Learning how to play?  Get started in Single player mode
  • Many muffins, receipts, and things to bake!

Friends offline at the moment?  Play against other people, our new dancing muffin bots (new v8.1), or try and beat the single player mode (new v8.0) and beat your best time!
