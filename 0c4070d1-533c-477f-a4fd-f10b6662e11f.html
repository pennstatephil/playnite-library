Description


Stellaxy is an Open World Procedural Space Exploration Universe Sandbox RPG Adventure Game with an infinite number of Alien Worlds to find!

Come explore a Universe teeming with life and treasures! Generated just for you! Stellaxy is a Single-Player game aimed at giving you, the player, your own world to explore and find new and exciting Adventures across the stars! Meet different species and learn all about their cultures, political climates, values, and more. Upgrade your ship to create the ultimate fighter and then give it a design to show off your creativity. Rebel against Dark and Evil not-so-secret alien societies and bring them to the Light of your Railgun. Liberate the Stars being harvested and free Planets from tyrannical rule. Trade and create a booming economy for Earth and it's people, and find Humanity's place among the Stars! Be a Completionist and 100% the game, or Speed Run it and share your fastest time! Complete Side Quests given to you by your Allies! Stellaxy is all about Free Will, play however you want to, the choice is yours.
