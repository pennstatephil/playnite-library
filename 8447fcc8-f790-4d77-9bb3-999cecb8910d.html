Description

Deep in the recesses of the digital world, the Explobers find themselves in perilous predicaments. Guide the Explobers to safety by blowing them up, building pathways, and navigating through dozens of fun, tricky puzzles and challenging platforming stages. Help them work together to achieve the seemingly impossible!

  • 80 challenging stages!
  • Unique multi-character single-player experience!
  • Expert goals for advanced players!
  • Unlock secret graphic modes and 20 bonus stages!
Explobers pushes your planning, puzzle and platforming skills to the limit for a challenging but rewarding experience. --MMOaholic

EXPLOBERS is available NOW in three different versions. Choose the one that's right for you!

EXPLOBERS Classic for Windows 7 and macOS. Playable with XInput controllers (recommended) or keyboard. 

Because of limited developer resources, compatibility with all editions of macOS cannot be guaranteed.  It has been verified to run on installations of macOS 10.13 (High Sierra) and macOS 12 (Monterey). See installation notes for more details.


EXPLOBERS GO is designed for Android-based handheld game consoles and Android phones with controller input only. It has been tested on and optimized for the Retroid Pocket 2 and requires Android 6.0 and up.  


EXPLOBERS Pocket (for Android 6.0 and up) is playable in portrait mode with touchscreen controls.
