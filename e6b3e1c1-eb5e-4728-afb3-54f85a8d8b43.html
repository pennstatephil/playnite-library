BAKED:MAGIC

[Fantasy, Romance, Puzzle,  GxG]



The story of a bakery, two witches, a knight, and some muffins. 

BAKED:MAGIC is a light-hearted story about Lana, a witch that owns a bakery. 
She recently took on an apprentice that is eager to learn how to bake delicious muffins using magic. 
They are frequented by their 'number one customer' who is a royal knight. 
Learn how Lana deals with love, life, rejection, and acceptance. 
Play through the four days of her life ending with a single big event.


Features:

Point and click mini games
Branching dialogues based on choices
Sound effects
Animated visuals
