About This Game

REVO is a high speed, neon drenched space shoot ‘em up inspired by top-down, vertical-scrolling arcade classics of the 1980’s. Dodge and shoot your way through waves of enemies, collect pickups, and unlock powerful, ammo hungry weapons. Defeat giant, shoot-the-core bosses to win your freedom.

Features

  • Dodge and shoot your way through 17 hand crafted levels.
  • Take on 10 classes of enemies and environmental hazards plus 5 boss battles.
  • Unlock powerful, ammo hungry weapons and equip up to four attacks at once.
  • Explore dozens of loadout combinations to suit a variety of play styles and scenarios.
  • Turn the debris of destroyed enemies into a surplus of resources that keep you in battle.
  • Enjoy an original, synth driven soundtrack and bask in neon glows.

Story

You’ve drifted into a sinister space storm far from civilization. Dark ships quickly close in as you are picked up by a mysterious symbiotic vessel. Your only hope of escape is to equip for combat and fight for your life.

Nostalgia

If you’re a fan of the coin-op video arcades and science fiction adventures of the early to mid 1980’s, you’ll love this game and find it both fresh and familiar. Inspirations include Galaga, Xevious, TRON, and The Last Starfighter.
