Description

In Ynglet, you jump, dash, slide, bounce and float your way through an abstract, effervescent sky. Melt into the reactive, dynamic soundtrack, while beautiful colors make every moment a visual treat.

A Floating non Platformer

In this experimental platforms, we did away with the most basic building block of the genre: The platforms.

Move to Groove

With a dynamic, reactive, beautifully complicated soundtrack, you'll make a new melody with every move you make.

Mesmerizing Movement

Learn new ways to wrap your mind around interlocking space, riding down slipstreams, dashing into reflective pads, and resting in safe, secure bubbles.

Doodles Come to Life

The hand-drawn art style evokes every absent-minded drawing you ever made in the margins of your school notebooks.

Fluid Difficulty

Quick respawns and granular difficulty means challenge comes in all shapes and sizes-- even the shape and size of an amoeboid squid-thing.


Minimum System Requirements

Windows

  • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
  • OS: Windows 7
  • Processor: 64-bit supporting AVX instructions (CPUs newer than ~2012 should support this)
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Dedicated graphics card
  • Storage: 1 GB available space
  • Visual Studio (2015, 2017, 2019) C++ Redistributable

Mac OS X

  • Requires MacOS 10.13 or above
  • Runs on Apple Silicon via Rosetta
