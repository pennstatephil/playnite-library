Description

This is a sizable collection of Touhou fangames created for the Touhou game jams. Our jam community has no tolerance for racism and strives to live in a Touhou fan community without it, so many of our creators have donated their jam entries here to be submitted to itch.io's Bundle for Racial Justice and Equality. Included as well is new content for NitorInc.: Touhou Microgames! DEMO+.

This project can be downloaded for free or with a donation tip. Tips will be split 50/50 between:

  • NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund
  • Community Bail Fund

EDIT: The bundle is now over, so this project is now available for free with an optional donation, which will continue to be forwarded to the same areas as the bundle.

Some of the games have exclusive updated content added after the jams as well. We hope you enjoy them and continue to show support for this cause.

Most downloads are for Windows. The individual game pages below may have a web version available however.
