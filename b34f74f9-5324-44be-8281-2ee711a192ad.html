Description

The Apollo Visualizer Kit

Tool for Unity3D that lets you easily create music visualizations without writing code. 

This tool for Unity 3D lets you read the intensity of 6 frequency ranges (Sub-bass, bass, low midrange, midrange, upper midrange and presence) or your own custom ranges on playing AudioSources in the scene in real time. This can be used to affect gameobjects in many ways like changing the the size, color, rotation or even emitting particles.

It comes with 7 built-in modifier scripts that will let you get started on making your visualizations and can serve as a starting point to build your own modifier.
