A NOTE FROM THE CREATOR

Hello. I am the Creator. I created this Visual Novel's script, code, and music.

The game runs in the RenPy engine.

It takes around two hours give or take to complete everything.

It is recommended that players are at least 12 years or older.
The game contains no jump scares or shock imagery.

The complete soundtrack is also available. It contains a little over an hour of music.

Thank you to SparkBag, Keeby, and Alexander A. McDonald for creating the majority of the visual assets.

Please enjoy, free of charge.

-The Creator.
